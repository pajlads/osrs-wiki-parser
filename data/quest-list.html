Miniquests

Miniquests are groups of objectives that, unlike Quests , have no icon on the world map and offer no Quest points upon completion. There will also be no indication of starting nor completing a miniquest, other than what is written in the corresponding Quest List journal entry. There are a total of 18 miniquests, which are all members -only.
